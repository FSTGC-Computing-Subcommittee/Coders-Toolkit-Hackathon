Scenario

Tina owns a restaurant called [Insert name].

The restaurant is open from Monday through Saturday from 8:00am to 8:00pm. The chefs and waiters work on shifts - a morning shift from 9:00am to 1:00pm and a night shift from 1:00pm to 8:00pm. If you wish to dine at the restaurant, you must call and make a reservation or you will have to wait until either someone misses their reservation or for a free time slot after you arrive. Once you are seated, a waiter or waitress will give you a menu to order from. You pay for what you ordered after your meal.

However, with the onset of the pandemic, regular operations have been disrupted.

Tina must find a a way to keep her restaurant afloat, can you find a soltuion for her?

Judging Criteria

All submissions will be judged based on the following:

  • Usability
  • Reliability
  • Creativity
  • Completeness

You can get a maximum for 10 points for each criterion for a total of 40 points.

Usability

The solution should be easy to use and easy to learn how to use.

Reliability

The solution should perform consistently. Try to keep bugs or erros to a minimum.

Creativity

The solution should be innovative.

Completeness

The solution should solve all aspects of the problems in the scenario.

Bonus

The following attract a bonus of 5 points each:
  • Using anything done in the Coder's Toolkit Workshops (Git, Linux, AWS, Docker)
  • Creating an object oriented solution
