Scenario

Sasha owns a restaurant called Kruger Cuisine, that's famous for its high quality steak meals.

The restaurant is open from Monday through Saturday from 8:00am to 8:00pm. The chefs and waiters work on shifts - a morning shift from 9:00am to 1:00pm and a night shift from 1:00pm to 8:00pm.

If a customer wishes to dine at the restaurant, they must call and make a reservation or they will have to wait until either someone misses their reservation or to wait for a free time slot after they arrive.

Once they are seated, a waiter or waitress will give them a menu to order from. They would then pay for what you ordered after receiving their meal.

However, with the onset of the pandemic, regular operations have been disrupted.

So, Sasha must find a a way to keep her restaurant afloat, can you find a solution for her?

Constraints

  • You have 3 hours to formulate and complete your solution
  • You must work in teams of 2-6 persons.

Judging Criteria

All submissions will be judged based on the following:

  • Usability
  • Reliability
  • Creativity
  • Completeness

Usability

The solution should be easy to use and easy to learn how to use.

Reliability

The solution should perform consistently. Try to keep bugs or erros to a minimum or come up with a fool-proof idea.

Creativity

The solution should be innovative.

Completeness

The solution should solve all aspects of the problems in the scenario.

You can get a maximum of 10 points for each criterion for a total of 40 points.

Bonus

The following attract a bonus of 5 points each:

  • Using anything done in the Coder's Toolkit Workshops (Git, Linux, AWS, Docker)
  • Creating an object oriented solution
